My Favorite Places to Travel

These are three of the places where I love to spend my time.

Hawaii

There are many sight-seeing places and beautiful views in Hawaii. My family and I go there every other year to relax on the beach and swim in the warm waters. We also like to visit the numerous waterfalls and caves.

India

A lot of my family still lives in India, so we go there every other year to visit them. I live with my cousins and we get to hang out. I also get to talk with my grandmas. In addition, there are many tourist spots like Goa and Taj Mahal.

Disneyland

There are many fun rides in Disneyland, as well as creative themes and yummy food. My family and I used to go there every other year when I was younger. After Disneyland, we used to go to Universal Studios as well, which is an hour or two away.
