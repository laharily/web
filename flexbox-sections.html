Looking Glass Falls

This is my favorite waterfall. It is along Hwy 276 in the Cradle of Forestry and Land of Waterfalls near Brevard NC. It can be viewed from a parking area right along the road. There is a distinctive rock formation around the waterfall.

Second Falls

This waterfall is in Graveyard Fields along the Blue Ridge Parkway. It is also one of my favorites.

Mt. Pisgah

This is a 5721 ft mountain on the Blue Ridge Parkway in my favorite section. There is an iconic TV tower on top that can be reached by a hiking trail to the summit.
