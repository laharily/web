Everyday Salad

Original recipe makes 1 bowl of salad.

Ingredients

  • fresh greens
  • english cucumber
  • red onion
  • nuts
  • dressing
    • olive oil
    • lemon juice
    • dijon mustard
    • garlic
    • sea salt
    • black pepper

Directions

  1. prepare the salad dressing -- whisk all of the dressing ingredients in a bowl until combined
  2. toss all the salad ingredients together in a large bowl until combined
  3. drizzle the dressing over the salad
  4. serve and enjoy!
